P5js 5: Optical Illusion

Reflection:

Description::
During the design phase, I created a sense of rhythm and movement that interacted with the viewer's perception of size and space. By resizing the logo to create variation, a sense of breathing is created, drawing the viewer's attention to both the static and dynamic aspects of the visual. The initial stage presents a large static logo, followed by a transition where multiple logos are gradually animated in a grid.

The background stories:
The figure I created has the head of one of the characters from the childhood Chinese animated classic "Pleasant Goat and Big Big Wolf" - Black Marshal. This cartoon was a huge hit and is still a favorite today. The body part is the Chinese national animal, the panda. Pandas are very cute and loved all over the world, even pandas can be used to promote diplomacy. The legs are bananas because I love bananas and bananas look like legs!

I used 'sin(time)' to adjust the size of the changes to be floating to ensure that the movement felt flexible rather than mechanical. I also experimented with a cascading effect using 'frame delay' to control when each part of the illusion appears.
